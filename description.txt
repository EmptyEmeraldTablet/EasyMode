Easy Mode Mod
=================

A mod that reduces game difficulty by controlling enemy attributes.

Features:
- Enemy movement speed reduced by 40%
- Boss movement speed reduced by 15%
- Enemy projectile speed reduced by 40%
- ~~Trap damage reduced by 30-50%~~ (Removed - damage is integer and cannot be scaled)
- Does NOT affect player attributes
- Friendly units and familiars are unaffected

This mod is designed for players who want a more relaxed gameplay experience without fundamentally changing the game's mechanics.

Compatible with all DLC content.
Does not affect achievements or progression.
